Independent Legal Advice in Ontario, What to Expect

ILA confirms you understand an agreement and its risks. We provide virtual appointments with fast turnaround.

When ILA is required

  • Private mortgages and refinancing
  • Guarantees or indemnities
  • Family transfers or title changes
  • Family agreements such as separation, cohabitation, or marriage contracts

How the meeting works

  1. Secure upload of photo ID and the draft agreement
  2. Thirty to forty five minute meeting in plain language
  3. Signing and delivery of the ILA certificate or acknowledgment
