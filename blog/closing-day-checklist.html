Closing Day Checklist for Ontario Home Buyers

A simple list to keep your purchase on track.

  • Send your signed Agreement of Purchase and Sale and status certificate if a condo
  • Provide ID, mortgage details, and lawyer contact info for the lender and realtor
  • Arrange home insurance effective on closing
  • Transfer down payment and closing funds by the deadline
  • Confirm key exchange with your realtor

We send status updates at each step and a final report after registration.
