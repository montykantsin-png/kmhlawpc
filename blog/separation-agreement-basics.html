Separation Agreements, What to Prepare

Preparation shortens timelines and keeps costs predictable.

  • Short summary of parenting schedule and decision making if applicable
  • Summary of property and debts on the separation date
  • Recent income documents such as T4, NOA, or pay statements
  • Any prior draft or term sheet to align expectations

For independent legal advice we can meet virtually and often return signed documents within forty eight hours after we receive the materials.
